About Us

The Prakhar Innovation Cell is established to foster creativity, entrepreneurial thinking, and problem-solving skills among engineering students. It aims to create a collaborative environment where students can transform ideas into innovative solutions, products, or startups, enhancing their technical and managerial abilities. A key focus of the cell is to raise awareness and facilitate activities related to Intellectual Property Rights IPR, helping students understand the importance of protecting and commercializing their innovations through patents, copyrights, and trademarks. Additionally, the Prakhar Innovation Cell works to enhance the branding of the department by showcasing its innovative achievements, promoting a culture of excellence, and building strong connections with industry and academia.
